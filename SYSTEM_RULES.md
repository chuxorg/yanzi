# SYSTEM_RULES

## Authority
- This document defines universal execution rules for all roles.
- Role packs may add guidance but cannot override these rules.

## Execution Discipline
- Prioritize small, verifiable changes.
- Keep decisions explicit, documented, and reviewable.

## Destructive Action Protection
- Avoid destructive operations unless explicitly approved.
- Prefer reversible actions and preserve recoverability.

## Semantic Versioning
- Follow semantic versioning for released artifacts.
- Document version-impact rationale for notable changes.

## Stability Guarantees
- Maintain behavioral stability for declared stable interfaces.
- Any breaking behavior must be explicit and intentional.

## Branch Protection
- Protected branches require review, checks, and traceable change history.
- Direct pushes to protected branches are disallowed unless policy explicitly permits.
