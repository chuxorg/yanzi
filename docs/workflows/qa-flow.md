# QA Flow

## Purpose
- Describe a minimal QA lifecycle for verification and readiness assessment.

## High-Level Lifecycle
- Intake candidate changes.
- Execute verification.
- Classify results (PASS/FAIL/WARN).
- Handoff certification status.

## Handoff Expectations
- Provide evidence-backed findings and unresolved risks.
- Clearly communicate certification disposition.

## Future Expansion Notice
- This is a baseline placeholder and will evolve with QA practice maturity.
