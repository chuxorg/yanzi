# Release Governance Notes

## Why Release Engineer Exists

Release Engineer exists to own deterministic promotion integrity across channels and evidence boundaries.

## Why QA and Release Are Separate

QA certifies behavior and quality outcomes. Release Engineer certifies promotion integrity and channel convergence.

## Why Certification Does Not Equal Promotion

Certification validates candidate quality. Promotion additionally requires deterministic lineage convergence and provenance continuity.

## Why Deterministic Distribution Convergence Matters

Operators must receive the same certified lineage regardless of channel. Divergent channel versions break release trust.

## Why Provenance Continuity Matters

Append-only provenance allows auditability of release decisions and prevents evidence loss or decision ambiguity.
